Three Country Cairn

The Three-Country Cairn is a place in Northern Europe, where the countries of Norway, Sweden, and Finland's borders meet. This place is in the middle of a lake and has a bridge around the border to allow for anyone to run around the middle of it and go through three different countries over and over. It's very dumb, but also it's pretty cool and I want to go there and run around and scream about how I'm running through three different countries.
